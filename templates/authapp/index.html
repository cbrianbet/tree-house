Property Management

MNEST brings all your properties in one platform. Tag them by location and the property manager managing them.

House Unit Management

MNEST allows you to view all occupied and vacant house units you have in a property. Change the unit status and attach a unit to a tenant.

Tenant Management

MNEST provides you an easy way of onboarding your tenants and continuous engagement through the platform.

Every tenant has a tenant facing application that allows them to post all rents paid.

Rent Management MNEST allows bank and mobile money integrations for best rent visibility and management. Once a tenant pays, the property owner is able to view by property and units and also issue rent invoices and receipts to a tenant.

Document Management

MNEST allows you to generate lease, invoices, receipts and automated rent due invoices to your tenants. Your tenants can also issue a notice to vacate your house and vice versa.

Service/Utility

MNEST allows you to add service providers to a property and updating of water and electrical bills for a unit.

Smart Homes

Our platform allows you to turn your house/property into a smart home. Call us learn more about this.
