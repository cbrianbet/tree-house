TERMS AND CONDITIONS

I. ACKNOWLEDGMENT

The Landlord agrees to rent the house to the Tenant under these terms in its present condition located at {{ tenant.unit.property.property_name }}, Kenya The Tenant acknowledges the terms of this Agreement and comply.

II. AGREEMENT TERM

This House Rental Agreement will begin on {{ tenant.created_at }} and end on ____________ . After this, a renewal agreement will be created for the new term.

III. MONTHLY RENTAL

The monthly rental shall be per month. Payments will be made by the date specified. The monthly rental shall be collected by every “Date of when on boarded/Number of Days”. If the tenant fails to pay on the due date, late charges of will be applied.

IV. SECURITY DEPOSIT

The Tenant agrees to pay a security deposit of KES. {{ tenant.unit.security_deposit }}. The Security Deposit is to be refunded upon leaving the residency, terminating this contract, and returning the keys to the Landlord/Agent. The Security Deposit will cover for any damage to the premise. This Security Deposit will also cover in case the Tenant fails to pay the utility bills.

V. UTILITIES

Tenants will be responsible for paying all utilities like electricity, water, gas, telephone, cable, internet and service charge.

VI. MAINTENANCE AND DAMAGES

Tenant shall keep the premises in good condition. The Tenant will be responsible for any damage caused which means the Tenant will bear all the expenses in fixing it. Tenants shall follow reasonable standards of cleanliness. Written permission from the “landlord/agent” is needed for any alterations. The Landlord may enter the premises for the purposes of inspection, repair, maintenance, and emergency.

VII. OCCUPANCY

The number of occupants is limited to “Number of Occupants”. It will only be occupied by the Tenant and his/her family members.

VIII. RULES and REGULATIONS

The number of guests shall not exceed 3. An overnight guest may not stay for more than two (2) nights. “Pets are not allowed on the premises” (other amenities). Smoking is prohibited on the premises.

IX. PEACE AND ORDER

Tenants are not allowed to keep firearms, bows, combat knife and other weapons on the premises. Tenants agree not to use the premises in a way to disturb the peace in the neighborhood.

X. ABANDONMENT

If the Tenant abandons the house, the Landlord/Agent has the right to enter the house by any means without liabilities. The Landlord is allowed to presume that Tenant abandoned the premise if the furniture and other items were removed. If the house is unoccupied for a period of 60 consecutive days without written notification to the Landlord, it will be considered as abandonment.

XI. AMENDMENTS

This Agreement can only be changed or modified through the written consent of both parties.

XII. GOVERNING LAW

This agreement shall be governed under the laws of the Kenya. We, the undersigned, hereby agreed that we have read this agreement and bounded by its terms and conditions.
